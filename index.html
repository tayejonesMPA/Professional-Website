01 — Profile
About Me

I am a senior government professional with over a decade of experience advising federal agencies, legislative bodies, and intergovernmental organizations on policy design, regulatory reform, and institutional accountability.

My work sits at the intersection of evidence and governance — translating complex research into actionable legislative strategy. I have served in both executive branch advisory roles and legislative staff positions, with deep expertise in budget reconciliation, oversight mechanisms, and cross-agency coordination.

I hold an M.P.A. from the Harvard Kennedy School and a B.A. in Political Science from Georgetown University. I am a member of the National Academy of Public Administration and a contributing fellow at the Brookings Institution.
